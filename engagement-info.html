🤝 Campaign Engagement

Campaign Engagement module tracks influencer participation, engagement performance and payout details for each marketing campaign.

About Engagement Management

This module connects influencers with campaigns and stores engagement scores, campaign status and payout information. It helps brands measure influencer contribution and campaign success.

Features

  • Track influencer campaign participation
  • Manage campaign engagement status
  • Store engagement scores
  • Calculate influencer payout details
  • Monitor campaign performance
  • Update engagement information

Workflow

Campaign created → Influencer assigned → Engagement recorded → Performance evaluated → Payout processed.
