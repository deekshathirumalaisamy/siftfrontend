About Campaign Management

SocialSift Campaign Management helps brands create, monitor and manage influencer marketing campaigns efficiently.

Users can create campaigns, assign influencers, manage budgets, track campaign status and analyze performance.

Campaign Creation

Create and store marketing campaigns with required details.

Budget Tracking

Manage campaign budgets and financial information.

Status Management

Track active, completed and pending campaigns.

Influencer Collaboration

Connect campaigns with suitable influencers.
